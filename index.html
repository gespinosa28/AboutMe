About Me

Hello! My name is Gina Espinosa and I am a web developer. One of the things that inspired me to pursue this career was the opportunity to create innovative technological products and experiences that people can connect with. From my point of view, a good product or experience should be intuitive, accessible, inclusive, and primarily focused on the customer needs, focused on providing value for them.

Even though I did not follow a traditional path as many others, my civil engineering background has given me set of unique and diverse skills such as adaptability, critical thinking, problem solving, attention to detail, and collaborative leadership which I firmly believe will provide value for any project I undertake.

Recently I have been working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript (ES6), React, NodeJS, ExpressJS, and Mongo DB as part of the curriculum of the MIT Professional Certificate in Coding.
